## LottoMiner

Check out my project, [LottoMiner](http://localhost:8090/#/portfolio/lotto-miner), in my Portfolio page.

I grew up tending to our family lotto ticket business, and this image is taken straight from the Google Maps Street View of our store in Cebu City.
It was a great business for our family until all of us kids grew up and started to pursue our own careers, we struggled to find time tending to that store.
And when my father died, we decided we had to close the store.
